About

Water quality is a decision, not a data dump.

Aqueduct was built for the people accountable for what comes out of the tap — utility operators, environmental labs and industrial safety teams. They don't need another dashboard of raw readings; they need a clear, defensible answer.

We pair rigorous modeling with restraint in the interface. The result is software that feels calm under pressure: fast to read, honest about uncertainty, and ready for the audit when it matters.
